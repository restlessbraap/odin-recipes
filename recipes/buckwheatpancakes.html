Buckwheat Pancakes

Description

Buckwheat is not a cereal, like wheat is. It is related to sorrel, knotweed, and rhubard, and is called a pseudocereal, because its uses are similar to other grains.

Buckwheat pancakes are heartier than white flour pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup buckwheat flour
  • 1/2 cup white flour
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups buttermilk

Instructions

  1. Mix together dry ingredients
  2. Mix wet ingredients into dry, saving the buttermilk for last.
  3. Ladle onto hot pan.
  4. Flip when bubbles rise to the surface and stay popped.
  5. Serve warm with butter and maple syrup.
