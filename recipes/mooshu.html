Mooshu Recipe

Description

Mooshu is a cabbage and carrot vegetable stirfry, which can have pork or beef added to it. The stirfry is wrapped up in thin pancakes and dipped in a molasses, peanut butter, and soy sauce mixture called hoisin sauce.

Mooshu is not difficult to make and is a nice change from stirfry with rice.

Ingredients

Stirfry:

  • Cabbage
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Eggs
  • Pork

Pancakes:

  • Flour
  • Water
